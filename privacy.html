Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Automagic Financial Sync ("we", "our", or "the Application") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service to connect with QuickBooks Online.

2. Information We Access

When you authorize the Application to connect to your QuickBooks Online account, we access:

  • Company information (name, address)
  • Financial transaction data (invoices, expenses, payments)
  • Account balances and reports
  • Customer and vendor information as needed for transaction context

3. How We Use Your Information

Your QuickBooks data is used solely to:

  • Synchronize transactions to your personal record-keeping system
  • Generate financial summaries and reports for your personal use
  • Identify patterns in your business finances

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is:

  • Processed locally on your own devices and systems
  • Not transmitted to external servers or third parties
  • Not sold or shared with any third parties
  • Protected by industry-standard security measures

5. Data Retention

Synchronized data remains in your personal systems under your control. We do not maintain copies of your financial data on external servers. You may delete your data at any time through your personal record-keeping system.

6. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with QuickBooks Online, which has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Intuit's privacy practices at intuit.com/privacy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through your personal systems
  • Delete any synchronized data from your systems
  • Revoke the Application's access to your QuickBooks account at any time through your QuickBooks Online settings
  • Request information about how your data is processed

8. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for use by children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
